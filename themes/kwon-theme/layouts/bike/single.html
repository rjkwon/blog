Parts list

I put this bike together over the course of about 3-4 years, accumulating parts from different places (mostly in the US). I was building it up slowly until my everyday ride was stolen (RIP) and probably sold at the Brick Lane
Component Make & model Weight Key specs Kwon notes
no image yet Saddle Cinelli Unicanitor 296 g Reissue of the classic, 7 mm rails This is a hard plastic saddle although some of the marketing materials call it "calibrated technopolymer" lol. It is quite rigid but much better for leaving outside in NYC weather (I usually bring it inside but sometimes I'm lazy and lock it up outside overnight), and not terrible comfort-wise if i wear padded shorts (and these days I don't really go for extended rides anyway).
